How to send an absentee ballot if you're going to be abroad https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/while-abroad/voting.html

Does voting even matter if I hate both/all candidates? Yes! Low voter turnout makes polarization even worse. Your vote makes a difference. https://arstechnica.com/science/2020/01/low-turnout-and-polarization-are-a-deadly-combo-for-electoral-stability/

Voting is really the minimum participation in democracy. You can have a much more interesting impact by letting your representatives know what issues are important to you.

Find your Representative https://www.house.gov/representatives/find-your-representative

Find your Senators https://www.senate.gov/general/contact_information/senators_cfm.cfm

National politics get the most air time, but if you're starting out, try getting involved locally. Go to town hall meetings, listen to your neighbors, run for local office.
